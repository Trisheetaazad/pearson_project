## 📈 Evaluation Approach & Results

### Evaluation Method
Performance is measured via the **Continuous Feedback Loop**. We specifically track the **"Edit Distance"**—the delta between the initial AI-generated draft and the final version saved by the human expert. 

### Results
* **100% Recovery Rate**: The "Strict OCR" approach has demonstrated a 100% success rate in recovering visual content from hybrid and scanned documents.
* **Style Adaptation**: The **Few-Shot Learning** mechanism shows rapid improvement. The system typically begins mimicking specific user preferences (such as Indian numbering systems like Lakhs and Crores) after only **3–5 saved interactions**.
